# Supplementary-files-for-our-ACM-survey-computing-review-paper
The supplementary files accompanying this ACM survey and computing review paper provide the complete structured dataset used in the study. The dataset includes 139 reviewed research entries related to Cyber-Physical-Social-Thinking (CPST) systems and their extensions within cyberspace and artificial general intelligence (AGI) contexts.

Each record is systematically encoded with standardized attributes, including study metadata (study ID, citation key, and publication year), research design, CPST dimensional coverage, AGI scope, architectural paradigm, cognitive level, and adaptation mechanisms. In addition, multiple quantitative assessment scores are provided, covering technological rigor, AGI relevance, innovation level, CPST relevance, and societal impact.

The supplementary materials are designed to ensure transparency, reproducibility, and extensibility of the survey methodology. They enable further statistical analysis, comparative studies, and future benchmarking of CPST-related research across different computational and intelligent system paradigms.
